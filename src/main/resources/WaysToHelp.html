How to Help Children in Need


If you’re wondering how to help children in need – please know that every little bit counts. A blanket. Clean water. Educational supplies for schools that have none. From America to Zimbabwe, Save the Children is there, helping one child at a time. But we can’t do it without you. Whether you give, volunteer or run a road race, your generosity means the world in the life of a child.
